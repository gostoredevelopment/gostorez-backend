# Campus GOSTOREz — Project Brief

**Purpose**  
Campus GOSTOREz connects university students and local vendors in Nigeria with a fast, mobile-friendly marketplace for buying and selling goods on campus.

**Target Users**  
- Students (buyers) at Nigerian universities  
- Small campus vendors and local sellers servicing students  

**Primary Features (Top 10)**  
1. Vendor profile creation and verification  
2. Product listing (images, price, variations)  
3. Search & browse by category and location  
4. Cart & checkout with payments (Paystack/Flutterwave)  
5. Order management and tracking  
6. In-app chat with images  
7. Video calls (Daily.co)  
8. Vendor dashboard (orders, products, balance)  
9. Wallet & balance management  
10. Hot Ads + subscription monetization  

**Brand Colors**  
- Main: `#9B4819`  
- Secondary: `#F4F1E8`  

**Monetization**  
- Vendor subscriptions (₦ 2,500 / month or ₦ 5,000 / year)  
- Paid Hot Ads (₦ 1,000 / 24 hrs)  
- Transaction fee per order  
- Premium verification badges  

**Success Metrics**  
1. 5,000 registrations in 90 days  
2. 2,000 fulfilled orders / month  
3. 10 % of vendors upgraded to paid plans  

**MVP Scope**  
Deliver fast browsing, listing, ordering, payments, and vendor dashboard + chat.
